Lentil Curry

Ready for plant-based flavor that your tastebuds actually crave?

By the time your done the kitchen aromas will haved transported you to New Delhi. Blend of fine curry spices and coconut milk with delicate lentils. This is your perfect combo of spicy meets sweet. Finally a vegan meal that is flavorful and filling. I always love the unique flavor profile of lentils. Pair with some rice and naan to cool down the heat and balance it out. Warning, don't blame us for the aftermath - Your gut microbes will be doing a happy dance later.

Ingredients

  • Red lentils
  • Coconut milk
  • Curry spice blend
  • Cilantro
  • Rice

Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil
  2. Add lentils. Cook on medium heat for 15 minutes or until tender. Make sure all the water is absorbed
  3. Remove from heat. Fill pan with remaining ingredients
  4. Simmer on low for 30 minutes
  5. Meanwhile prepare your rice
  6. When rice is finished serve and top with cilantro
  7. Enjoy!
