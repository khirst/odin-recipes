Instant Ramen

Just Add Hot Water

Really, that's all there is to it! Boil some water and you will be moments away from godly flavor.

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen bowl
  • Boiling water

Instructions

  1. Follow package instructions. Typically first you tear open spice packet and add to noodles
  2. Then you pour boiling water to fill line of cup
  3. Let sit for 5 minutes (or whatever the package says)
  4. Itadakimasu!
