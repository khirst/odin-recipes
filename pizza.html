Pizza - pungent, but so addictive

One of Lord Beerus all time favorite dishes from all his journeys throughout space and time. Starting from the top: the substance "cheese" is melty, gooey, delectable. Beneath the cheese is a simple yet full-flavored tomato sauce. Underneath it all you'll find a soft doughy crust baked to perfection. If homemade isn't your style earth subjects are just a phonecall away for delivery. However, the 10 mile radius delivery limit probably won't cover deep space.


Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings of your choice! The possibilities are endless.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Let pizza dough rise at room temperature
  2. Flour a surface and roll out dough into a circle
  3. Add a little bit of olive oil followed by tomato sauce.
  4. If your feeling fancy, add some basil leaves for extra flavor.
  5. Sprinkle on that cheese
  6. Add any toppings
  7. Pop it in the oven for 15 minues and get ready for the ooey gooey flavor
