Sushi

The dish that put global annihilation on hold.

This recipe is a much more complex and not for the faint of heart. Again, takeout is always an option.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Raw Salmon Slices
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi (demon topping)

Instructions

  1. Place the rice on a strip of seaweed
  2. Add the salmon to the end
  3. Roll the seaweed strip up
  4. Repeat with remainder of ingredients
